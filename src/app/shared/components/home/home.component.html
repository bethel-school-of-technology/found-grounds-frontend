Found Grounds

THE APP

We love good coffee

Found Grounds is an interactive experience for coffee lovers. Social media for everything coffee. Have ever been traveling or have been out in the town and just craved a good cup of coffee? So like anyone these day you pull out phone and search for the nearest coffee shop and a less than exciting gas station chain or fast food restaurants populate your feed. Found Grounds wants to change that. Coffee lovers can rate and submit their favorite coffee shops so other people can see what others have had and what they recommend from their favorite shops. This app is brewed to perfection!
